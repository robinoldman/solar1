Save money

Generating your own electricity can lead to substantial savings on your utility bills, especially considering that the current unit cost of electricity has surpassed 20 pence per kilowatt-hour (kWh) for many consumers. By producing your electricity for free, you can effectively reduce your energy expenses.

Reduce environmental impact

Based on the specific scale of your solar energy setup and where you're located, there's the potential for annual carbon savings of up to 1.5 metric tons. If you decide to integrate energy storage solutions or make the transition to an electric vehicle, you can further enhance your positive impact on the environment. Embracing these choices will not only reduce your carbon footprint but also actively contribute to eco-conscious practices.

Become Energy Independent

Shield yourself from the unpredictability of energy prices while lessening your dependence on grid-supplied electricity. This approach helps you establish a more secure and stable energy f uture for your household. By harnessing your own renewable energy sources, you gain a degree of insulation against the ups and downs of energy costs, and you become less vulnerable to external fluctuations in the energy market.

Make Money from Energy Exports

Enhance your financial gains by capitalizing on opportunities like the SEG (Smart Export Guarantee) or enrolling in an energy trading initiative. The SEG allows you to earn income by exporting surplus energy back to the grid, ensuring you receive compensation for your contributions. This not only makes your solar investment more economically rewarding but also aligns with sustainable energy practices. Additionally, with the incorporation of energy storage in the form of batteries, you can tap into intelligent pricing structures such as Octopus Agile. This approach lets you maximize savings by using and storing electricity during periods of lower costs, subsequently reducing your reliance on more expensive grid power during peak times.
